**Haodong Lyu** is a postgraduate student at Key Laboratory of Ecological Safety and Sustainable Development in Arid Lands, Xinjiang Institute of Ecology and Geography. He is currently pursuing master's degree at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. His research mainly focuses on hydrological modelling and projection.
